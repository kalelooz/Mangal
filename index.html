Our Story

From the heart of Turkey to the vibrant community of Al Wukair

A Journey of Flavor

Mangal was born from a dream: to share the rich, authentic flavors of our homeland with the world. Our journey began in the bustling markets and family kitchens of Turkey, where recipes are passed down through generations. Each dish we prepare is a tribute to this heritage.

We bring the soul of Turkish cuisine to Al Wukair, Qatar, using time-honored cooking methods and the freshest ingredients. At Mangal, you're not just a customer; you're our guest. Welcome to our family.
